Support Our Mission

Your contribution helps GAIDI empower communities through health, education, livelihoods, research and disability inclusion.

Why Your Support Matters

Every contribution to GAIDI strengthens our ability to reach vulnerable communities, promote inclusive development, support persons with disabilities, improve community wellbeing and create opportunities for sustainable transformation.

Whether you give financially, donate materials, sponsor a program or become a monthly supporter, your contribution helps us move closer to our vision of empowered, resilient and inclusive communities.

Health & Wellbeing

Support community health education, disease prevention, WASH, nutrition, mental health and outreach activities.

Education & Research

Support mentorship, digital literacy, learning materials, research, innovation and evidence generation.

Livelihoods

Support agriculture, entrepreneurship, vocational skills, financial literacy and household economic resilience.

Disability Inclusion

Support sign language promotion, accessibility, disability rights advocacy, inclusive education and empowerment.

Donation Accountability

GAIDI values transparency, accountability and responsible stewardship of all resources. Every donation pledge submitted through this page is recorded for follow-up, verification and internal accountability.

Step 1

Make or pledge your donation through mobile money, bank transfer, cash or in-kind support.

Step 2

Fill in the donation record form so that GAIDI can verify and acknowledge your support.

Step 3

GAIDI reviews and confirms the donation record from the administrative dashboard.

Step 4

Your contribution supports programs that directly benefit communities.

Donation Pledge / Record Form

Kindly complete this form after donating or pledging support. Your submission will be received by GAIDI for verification, acknowledgement and accountability tracking.
