About GAIDI

Building inclusive, resilient and self-reliant communities through integrated development.

Who We Are

Global All Integrated Development Initiative (GAIDI) is a non-profit, non-political, non-partisan and non-denominational community development organization headquartered in Gulu City, Uganda.

GAIDI exists to empower vulnerable and underserved communities through integrated, innovative and community-driven interventions in health, livelihoods, education, research and disability inclusion.

The organization is guided by the belief that sustainable development requires inclusive participation, accountable leadership, evidence-based programming and strong partnerships.

Our Vision

To be a leading catalyst for inclusive and sustainable socio-economic transformation among vulnerable and underserved communities.

Our Mission

To empower vulnerable populations through integrated, innovative and community-driven interventions in health, livelihoods, education, research and disability inclusion.

Why GAIDI Exists

Many communities continue to face barriers related to poverty, disability, poor health, unemployment, limited access to education, weak livelihoods, gender inequality and social exclusion.

GAIDI was established to respond to these challenges through integrated development approaches that combine community mobilization, skills building, advocacy, research, partnerships and practical interventions.

Our Governance Approach

GAIDI promotes accountable and transparent governance through a structured institutional framework consisting of the General Assembly, Board of Trustees, Executive Committee, Executive Director, Secretariat and Standing Committees.

This structure ensures separation between governance, oversight and daily management while promoting professionalism, institutional continuity and donor confidence.
