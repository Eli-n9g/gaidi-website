Membership

Become part of a movement dedicated to inclusive and sustainable community development.

Why Become a Member?

Membership in GAIDI provides an opportunity to contribute to governance, growth and sustainability while supporting community empowerment, disability inclusion, education, health and livelihoods.

Members serve as ambassadors of the organization and contribute ideas, expertise, resources and networks that strengthen our impact.

Individual Membership

Open to individuals who support the mission, vision and objectives of GAIDI.

Institutional Membership

Open to NGOs, CBOs, academic institutions, private sector organizations and community groups wishing to collaborate with GAIDI.

International Membership

Open to supporters, professionals, researchers and partners from outside Uganda.

Honorary Membership

Awarded to distinguished individuals who have made exceptional contributions to community development and GAIDI’s objectives.

Member Benefits

Participation

Participate in meetings, consultations and organizational activities.

Networking

Connect with professionals, institutions and development partners.

Leadership

Opportunity to serve in governance and leadership structures.

Learning

Access to capacity-building opportunities and organizational updates.
