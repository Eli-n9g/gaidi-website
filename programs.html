Our Programs

Integrated, evidence-informed and community-driven interventions for sustainable transformation.

GAIDI Programmatic Framework

GAIDI implements its work through four strategic programmatic pillars designed to address interconnected community challenges. Our approach recognizes that health, livelihoods, education, innovation, research and disability inclusion must work together to create sustainable impact.

🏥

Health & Wellbeing

This pillar promotes equitable access to health information, preventive services and community wellbeing initiatives.

Key Focus Areas

  • Community health education and awareness
  • Disease prevention and control
  • Maternal, newborn, child and adolescent health
  • Sexual and reproductive health education
  • Nutrition and food security promotion
  • Water, sanitation and hygiene (WASH)
  • Mental health and psychosocial support
  • Emergency preparedness and humanitarian response
🌱

Livelihoods & Economic Empowerment

This pillar supports poverty reduction, household resilience, entrepreneurship and sustainable income generation.

Key Focus Areas

  • Agriculture and agribusiness development
  • Climate-smart farming practices
  • Vocational and technical skills development
  • Youth economic empowerment
  • Women’s economic empowerment
  • Financial literacy and savings culture
  • Cooperative and community enterprise development
  • Market linkages and value addition
📚

Education, Innovation & Research

This pillar advances access to learning, digital literacy, innovation, research and evidence-based development.

Key Focus Areas

  • Educational support and mentorship
  • Career guidance and life skills development
  • Digital literacy and ICT skills
  • Science, technology and innovation promotion
  • Community-based research and evidence generation
  • Training, coaching and capacity building
  • Innovation challenges and competitions
  • Publications, reports and knowledge sharing
♿

Disability Inclusion & Empowerment

This pillar promotes dignity, accessibility, participation, rights and socio-economic empowerment of persons with disabilities.

Key Focus Areas

  • Disability rights advocacy
  • Sign language training and inclusive communication
  • Inclusive education support
  • Accessibility and universal design promotion
  • Economic empowerment of persons with disabilities
  • Talent identification and development
  • Disability-sensitive research
  • Disability mainstreaming across all GAIDI programs
