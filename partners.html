Partnerships & Support

Collaborating for inclusive, sustainable and community-driven development.

Why Partner With GAIDI?

GAIDI welcomes partnerships with individuals, institutions, development partners, private sector actors, academic institutions and community structures that share our commitment to inclusive and sustainable development.

Our governance framework emphasizes accountability, transparency, safeguarding, disability inclusion, community participation and evidence-based programming.

💰 Financial Support

Support community projects through grants, donations, sponsorships, fundraising support and program financing.

🧠 Technical Support

Provide expertise in public health, agriculture, education, disability inclusion, research, governance, technology and organizational development.

🤝 Strategic Partnerships

Collaborate with GAIDI on projects, advocacy initiatives, training programs, research, community outreach and institutional strengthening.

🎓 Research Collaboration

Partner with us on community-based research, innovation, evidence generation, policy briefs, publications and learning initiatives.

🙋 Volunteer Engagement

Contribute your skills, time and experience to support vulnerable communities through volunteer service and professional mentorship.

🏢 Corporate Social Responsibility

Work with GAIDI through CSR initiatives that promote health, education, livelihoods, disability inclusion and community resilience.

Potential Partner Categories

Government

Local governments, ministries and public institutions.

NGOs & CSOs

Local, national and international civil society organizations.

Academia

Schools, universities, researchers and training institutions.

Private Sector

Businesses, foundations and corporate social responsibility actors.

Our Partnership Principles

All GAIDI partnerships are guided by mutual respect, transparency, accountability, shared values, equity, ethical conduct and measurable impact.

We value partners who respect community dignity, promote inclusion, strengthen local ownership and support sustainable solutions.

Let’s Build Stronger Communities Together

Partner with GAIDI to support vulnerable populations, strengthen livelihoods, promote inclusion and create sustainable community impact.
