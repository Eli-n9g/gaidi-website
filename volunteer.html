Volunteer With GAIDI

Use your time, skills and passion to empower communities and promote inclusive development.

Why Volunteer With Us?

Volunteers are an important part of GAIDI’s mission. Through volunteering, individuals can contribute their knowledge, skills, creativity and compassion to support vulnerable communities, strengthen local capacity and promote sustainable change.

Whether you are a student, professional, researcher, community worker, health worker, educator, farmer, communicator or development practitioner, GAIDI provides opportunities to serve meaningfully across our programmatic pillars.

Health & Wellbeing

Support health education, community sensitization, nutrition promotion, WASH awareness, mental health support and health outreach activities.

Livelihoods & Agriculture

Contribute to agricultural training, entrepreneurship mentorship, financial literacy, vocational skills and community enterprise development.

Education & Research

Support mentorship, career guidance, digital literacy, data collection, community research, documentation and knowledge-sharing initiatives.

Disability Inclusion

Support sign language training, accessibility awareness, disability rights, inclusive education, talent development and safeguarding activities.

Communications & Media

Help with photography, videography, social media, website updates, storytelling, branding, newsletters and public engagement.

Administration & Events

Support community mobilization, event coordination, registration, reporting, logistics, office administration and stakeholder engagement.

Who Can Volunteer?

Students

University, tertiary and secondary students seeking service and learning opportunities.

Professionals

Health workers, educators, researchers, IT experts, agriculturalists and development workers.

Community Members

Individuals passionate about local development, inclusion and community transformation.

International Volunteers

Global supporters interested in technical assistance, mentorship, research and partnerships.

Volunteer Principles

Integrity

Volunteers are expected to serve honestly, respectfully and responsibly.

Safeguarding

All volunteers must protect children, vulnerable adults and community members from harm.

Inclusion

Volunteers must respect diversity and promote meaningful participation for all.

Accountability

Volunteers shall follow GAIDI policies, report responsibly and protect organizational resources.
