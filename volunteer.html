Volunteer With GAIDI

Use your time, skills and passion to support inclusive community development.

Why Volunteer With Us?

Volunteers are an important part of GAIDI’s mission. Through volunteering, individuals can contribute knowledge, skills, creativity and compassion to support vulnerable communities and promote sustainable change.

Whether you are a student, professional, researcher, community worker, health worker, educator, farmer, communicator or development practitioner, GAIDI provides opportunities to serve meaningfully.

Health & Wellbeing

Support health education, community sensitization, nutrition promotion, WASH awareness, mental health support and outreach activities.

Livelihoods & Agriculture

Contribute to agricultural training, entrepreneurship mentorship, financial literacy, vocational skills and community enterprise development.

Education & Research

Support mentorship, career guidance, digital literacy, data collection, documentation and learning initiatives.

Disability Inclusion

Support sign language training, accessibility awareness, disability rights, inclusive education and safeguarding activities.

Communications & Media

Help with photography, videography, social media, website updates, storytelling, branding and newsletters.

Administration & Events

Support mobilization, events, registration, reporting, logistics, administration and stakeholder engagement.

Who Can Volunteer?

Students

University, tertiary and secondary students seeking service and learning opportunities.

Professionals

Health workers, educators, researchers, IT experts, agriculturalists and development workers.

Community Members

Individuals passionate about local development, inclusion and transformation.

International Volunteers

Global supporters interested in technical assistance, mentorship and research.
