Welcome to GAIDI

Global All Integrated Development Initiative (GAIDI) is a community-driven organization committed to empowering vulnerable and underserved populations through integrated interventions in health, education, livelihoods, disability inclusion, research and innovation.

We believe that sustainable development is achieved when communities are empowered to participate in decisions that affect their lives and when no one is left behind.

Who We Are

Established to address the evolving needs of vulnerable and underserved populations, GAIDI works collaboratively with communities, institutions, government agencies, development partners and civil society actors to promote sustainable development, resilience and social inclusion.

We believe every individual deserves the opportunity to live a healthy, productive and dignified life regardless of disability, gender, social status, ethnicity or economic background.

Our Vision

To be a leading catalyst for inclusive and sustainable socio-economic transformation among vulnerable and underserved communities.

Our Mission

To empower vulnerable populations through integrated, innovative and community-driven interventions in health, livelihoods, education, research and disability inclusion.

Our Programmatic Pillars

🏥 Health & Wellbeing

Health promotion, disease prevention, nutrition, WASH, mental health and community health strengthening.

🌱 Livelihoods & Economic Empowerment

Agriculture, entrepreneurship, vocational skills development, financial literacy and economic resilience.

📚 Education, Innovation & Research

Education support, digital literacy, innovation, research and knowledge generation.

♿ Disability Inclusion & Empowerment

Sign language promotion, disability rights, accessibility, inclusion and talent development.
